Our Story

Redefining Cleanliness in the UAE

Founded on Swiss-standard cleanliness principles, My Clean Home is Abu Dhabi's premier luxury cleaning service.

The Vision Behind My Clean Home

Launched in Abu Dhabi, My Clean Home was born out of a simple observation: while cleaning services were widely available, the premium quality, trust, security, and meticulous attention to detail expected by luxury homeowners were missing.

We built a company that approaches house cleaning with the discipline, training, and customer service standards of a 5-star hotel. Today, we service villas, penthouses, and executive offices across all seven Emirates.
